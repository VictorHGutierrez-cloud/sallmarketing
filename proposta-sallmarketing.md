## 02 — Context
Marketing company in **Georgia, USA** with remote team members in **Morocco** and other countries. **19 employees**, flexible schedules — **no time tracking** required. First HR platform evaluation. Currently: Google Drive, email, Google Sheets, separate e-signature, manual recruitment tracking. Campaign KPIs remain in **Go High Level**.

## 03 — Client priorities (discovery + demo)
1. Per-employee document organization (resumes, IDs, diplomas, policies)
2. ATS / recruitment (LinkedIn, Indeed, Facebook, Glassdoor) — replace email + sheets
3. Role-based onboarding (directors vs content creators) with SOPs and trainings
4. Time off (vacation, sick, maternity) with manager approval — no clock in/out
5. Payslip distribution (bulk PDF split per employee vs 19 emails)
6. **Expenses for 5 users** — AI/creative tool reimbursements (Cursor, Claude, etc.)
7. Org chart, e-signatures, Factorial One AI reports

## 05 — Solution highlights (demo)
- Recruitment: job posts, pipeline, WhatsApp, Google Meet scheduling, offer letter + sign
- Onboarding: document upload folders, custom tasks, policies, fillable PDFs, bank details
- Time Off: allowances, approvals, Factorial One time-off reports
- Payslips: bulk upload → auto-split to each employee
- Trainings: onboarding paths for new hires
- Expenses: receipts, multi-currency, approval workflow for 5 users

## 06 — Payroll
Third-party payroll / bookkeeper — Factorial does not process payroll; syncs and distributes payslips.
